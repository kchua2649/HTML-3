Musical Genres

Music is an a hodge podge of genres than can only seem to keep growing as time moves on.

What do genres do?

Genres are basically different styles and sounds that are
categorized into their "main" forms, then into sub-genres.

Categorizing by Ear

It may not be too hard to point out the general genre of a piece of music,
but the real challenge comes from identifying the sub genre.

The point of sub-genres

Sub genres, like any sub category, help to differentiate sounds more precisely than the "main" forms.
